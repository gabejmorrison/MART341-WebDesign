No-Knead Ciabatta Bread


Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups white bread flour
  • .5 cups whole wheat bread flour
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • .25 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons cornmeal

Directions

  1. Add flours, salt, yeast, and water to a bowl. Mix until dough is cohesive. Cover with foil, let rise for 18 hours at room temperature.
  2. Fold dough over itself
  3. Sprinkle cornmeal over lightly greased baking sheet
  4. Sprinkle flour on dough. Gently shape it to be long, flat, and rectangular. Move dough to pan. Dust with flour, cover it with a light towel, and let rise an additional 2 hours.
  5. Bake 35-45 minutes in a 425 degree F oven
  6. For more information on the original recipe visit: allrecipes.com
